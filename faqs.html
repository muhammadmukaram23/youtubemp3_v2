Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our YouTube to MP3 converter

C3MP is a free online service that allows you to convert YouTube videos to MP3 audio files. Simply paste a YouTube URL, select your desired audio quality, and our server will process the video and provide you with a downloadable MP3 file. The process is completely free and doesn't require any software installation.

Yes, C3MP is completely free to use. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or premium plans. You can convert as many YouTube videos to MP3 as you want without any cost.

We offer four quality options:

  • Low (96kbps): Smaller file size, suitable for basic listening
  • Medium (128kbps): Good balance between quality and file size
  • High (192kbps): High quality audio for music enthusiasts
  • Ultra (320kbps): Maximum quality for audiophiles

Conversion time depends on the video length and current server load. Most videos are converted within 1-3 minutes. Longer videos or high-quality conversions may take a bit more time. You can monitor the progress in real-time on our interface.

No registration or account creation is required. You can start converting YouTube videos to MP3 immediately without providing any personal information.

Common reasons for conversion failure include:

  • Invalid or private YouTube URL
  • Video is too long (over 1 hour)
  • Video has copyright restrictions
  • Temporary server overload
  • Network connectivity issues

Try again with a different video or check your internet connection.
